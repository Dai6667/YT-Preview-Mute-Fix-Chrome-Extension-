# Privacy Policy

YT Preview Mute Fix does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data.

## What the extension does

- Runs only on YouTube pages ("youtube.com" and related domains) to force-mute hover/thumbnail preview videos.

## Data collection

- No analytics
- No tracking
- No cookies
- No external network requests

## Contact

If you publish this extension, add your contact email or support URL in the Chrome Web Store listing.
